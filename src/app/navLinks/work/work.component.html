Flavorique

MongoDB Express.js Angular Node.js

Flavorique is a full-stack MEAN web application that allows users to explore, post, and manage their uploaded recipes. The platform includes user authentication, recipe creation and editing, image upload support, and category-based browsing. Visitors view and add recipes to cart. The intuitive interface ensures smooth navigation, making it easy to discover and share delicious ideas with a wider community.
